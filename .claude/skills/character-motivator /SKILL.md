
# **Character motivator skill**

This skill analyzes a character’s actions, decisions, and dialogue to provide insights into their motivations and personality consistency throughout the story.

## **Usage**

Provide a description of a character’s actions or plot points. The skill identifies whether the behavior aligns with the character’s traits and goals.

**Example:**

**User:**
"Analyze this character: 'Liam donates all his money to charity but secretly envies his wealthy friends.'"

**Assistant:** *(Invokes character motivator skill)*

**Skill Output:**

```
Observation: Liam’s charitable actions suggest altruism, but his envy indicates internal conflict. Consider highlighting this duality consistently to make his motivations believable.
```
