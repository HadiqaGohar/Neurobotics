
# **Grammar and Style Enhancer Skill**

This skill improves the grammar, tone, and clarity of your writing. It identifies issues and provides refined suggestions for:

* **Grammar Errors:** Punctuation, capitalization, verb tense consistency, subject–verb agreement, and sentence structure.
* **Stylistic Issues:** Wordiness, passive voice, repetitive phrases, awkward wording, and clichés.
* **Clarity & Conciseness:** Rewriting or restructuring sentences for better readability and understanding.

## **Usage**

To use this skill, simply provide the text you want improved. The skill analyzes your content and returns corrected or enhanced suggestions.

---

## **Example**

**User:**
"Please improve the grammar and style of this text: 'She go to market everyday and buy many thing. It make her happy.'"

**Assistant:** *(Invokes Grammar and Style Enhancer skill)*

**Skill Output:**

```
Original: She go to market everyday and buy many thing. It make her happy.

Suggestions:
- Correct verb forms: "go" → "goes", "buy" → "buys", "make" → "makes"
- Fix pluralization: "thing" → "things"
- Improve clarity and flow:
  "She goes to the market every day and buys many things. It makes her happy."
```
