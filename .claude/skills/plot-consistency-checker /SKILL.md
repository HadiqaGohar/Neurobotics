
# **Plot Consistency Checker Skill**

This skill helps ensure your story maintains logical flow and narrative coherence. It identifies potential inconsistencies, contradictions, or gaps in timelines, character actions, and event sequences.

## **Usage**

Provide a summary of your story or key plot points. The skill analyzes the information and flags areas that may need revision to maintain consistency.

---

## **Example**

**User:**
"Check the plot consistency for my story: 'Emma, a scientist, invents a time machine. Later, she is shocked to discover that time travel is impossible.'"

**Assistant:** *(Invokes Plot Consistency Checker skill)*

**Skill Output:**

```
Potential inconsistency detected:
- Emma successfully invents a time machine, but later expresses disbelief that time travel is possible.
- To resolve this, clarify whether her invention failed, was a dream, or if there is another explanation for her surprise.
```
